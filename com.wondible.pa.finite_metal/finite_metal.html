Finite Metal
Metal spots are reclaimable for 2000 metal each. Use area commands to reclaim.
Metal "Markers" exist only to help route fabbers to spots.
Bug: Reclaim rate is limited by build rate - if build speed is being attenuated by lack of metal, reclaim rates will also be reduced, exacerbating the problem. You must stop some spending to let reclaim rates recover.
Bug: If a metal spot is reclaimed, the strategic icon stays, but you can't build or reclaim it anymore (very rarely a fabber will lucky when reclaiming-to-build)
This mod doesn't include changes to the commander, so you do have a base income to work with.
